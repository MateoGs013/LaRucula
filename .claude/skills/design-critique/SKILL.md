# Design Critique

If Playwright screenshots exist in `tests/artifacts/`, review those alongside the code.

Review the current section or page and answer:

1. What feels generic?
2. What feels too safe?
3. Where is the hierarchy weak?
4. Is the layout too card-heavy or too centered?
5. What is the strongest visual move?
6. What one change would make it feel more authored and memorable?

Rules:
- be specific
- do not praise weak work
- focus on composition, hierarchy, and rhythm
- prefer one strong redesign move over many small suggestions
