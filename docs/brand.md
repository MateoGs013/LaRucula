# LaRucula — Brand Foundation

## Brand name
LaRucula

## Brand essence
LaRucula is a premium beachfront restaurant in Spain with an identity that feels warm, artistic, Mediterranean, refined, and contemporary.

It should feel like:
- a curated place
- a sensorial dining experience
- a refined but relaxed atmosphere
- a restaurant with authorship and aesthetic criteria
- a coastal destination with quiet luxury

## Emotional qualities
- calm
- tactile
- elegant
- sun-warmed
- intimate
- timeless
- human
- editorial

## What the brand is not
- not generic
- not touristy
- not corporate
- not loud
- not trendy for the sake of trend
- not rustic in a cliché way
- not excessively ornamental

## Visual vocabulary
- editorial compositions
- asymmetry with balance
- premium whitespace
- refined serif typography
- understated sans-serif UI
- subtle handwritten accents
- image-first storytelling
- warm, natural materials
- coastal atmosphere without clichés

## Color palette
Primary palette:
- sand beige
- ivory
- toasted brown
- stone gray

Possible tonal role suggestions:
- Sand Beige: base backgrounds, soft warmth
- Ivory: elevated light surfaces, text contrast support
- Toasted Brown: emphasis, grounding, CTA accents, editorial blocks
- Stone Gray: structure, subtle contrast, quiet framing

## Typography direction
- Display serif for large headings and editorial moments
- Clean sans-serif for navigation, interface, labels, and body text
- Handwritten or cursive accents only in small doses

## Content tone
The tone should feel:
- refined
- calm
- atmospheric
- confident
- understated
- evocative, not exaggerated

## Brand keywords
Mediterranean / editorial / premium / artistic / coastal / tactile / refined / quiet luxury / hospitality / warm minimalism
